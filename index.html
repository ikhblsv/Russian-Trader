Methodology

How I trade

01 Market Structure
Before opening any trade, I identify the market phase — trend, correction, or consolidation. This is the foundation. 80% of traders lose money because they trade against structure. I teach you to read the market the way institutional players do.
02 Key Levels
Not indicators — support and resistance levels. Zones where real money sits. I mark them on higher timeframes and wait for price reaction. No guessing — only confirmed zones of interest.
03 Entry Points
I enter only when at least 3 factors align: structure + level + Price Action pattern. This filters out 90% of noise. One quality trade per day beats ten random entries every time.
04 Risk Management
2% max per trade. Always. Stop-loss is mandatory. No stop — no trade. Risk management is what separates a professional from a gambler. This rule has protected my capital through every market crash.
05 Psychology
A strategy without discipline is nothing. I teach emotional control — don't revenge trade, don't size up after a loss. Trade journal, checklists, routines — all part of the system that actually makes money.
06 Multi-Timeframe
Top-down analysis: Monthly → Weekly → Daily → H4 → H1. The higher timeframe sets direction, the lower one gives the entry. This eliminates counter-trend trades and pushes accuracy to 90%.
